What is a Time Capsule?

A Time Capsule is a container or digital space where messages, memories, images, or important information are stored and sealed, to be opened at a specific time in the future.

Traditionally, time capsules were buried underground or sealed in buildings. Today, technology allows us to create digital time capsules that are safer, more reliable, and accessible from anywhere in the world.

How Can You Use This Website?

  • Create a personal digital time capsule
  • Add text messages, photos, or letters with Emojis
  • Select a future opening date
  • Lock the capsule securely
  • Open it only when the choosen time arrives

What Can Users Do?

Users can store memories for:

  • Future self-messages
  • Birthday or anniversary surprises
  • Messages to loved ones
  • Personal goals and reminders
  • Historical or emotional records

How Secure Is Time Capsule?

Security is our top priority. Every capsule is protected using:

  • End-to-end encryption
  • Secure authentication
  • Time-based access control
  • No third-party access

Only the creator can access the capsule, and only at the selected time.
